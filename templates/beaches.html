BEACHES

Planning a beach vacation offers the perfect escape to relax and rejuvenate by the sea. Start by selecting a destination that suits your preferences, considering factors like travel budget, amenities, and activities available. Pack essentials such as sunscreen, swimwear, and beach gear. Book accommodations in advance, ideally close to the beach, and research local attractions to enhance your trip. Embrace the leisurely pace of beach life, indulge in fresh seafood, and make time for water sports and sunset strolls.

  • Baga Beach, Goa
  • Kovalam Beach, Kerala
  • Palolem Beach, Goa
  • Marina Beach, Tamil Nadu
  • Varkala Beach, Kerala
  • Anjuna Beach, Goa
